## Key Outcomes

The group aligned on launching an **Instagram-based marketing campaign** tied to a **Kickstarter fundraiser** for an upcoming short film. Rather than marketing the plot, the strategy is to sell the film's **aesthetic vibe** through curated photos and short videos.
